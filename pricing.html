Yes! You can use our Starter plan completely free for 3 months. No credit card required to sign up.

Absolutely. You can upgrade, downgrade, or switch to commission-based pricing at any time.

For subscription payments: M-Pesa, Tigo Pesa, Airtel Money, Visa, and Mastercard. For customer payments: All major Tanzanian mobile money providers and cards.
