Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing and plans

Is there really a 3-month free trial?

Yes! Our Starter plan is completely free for the first 3 months. No credit card required to sign up. After 3 months, you can choose to continue with the Starter plan at TZS 4,999/month, upgrade to Professional, or switch to our commission-based model.

Can I switch between plans?

Absolutely. You can upgrade, downgrade, or switch to commission-based pricing at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any differences for the remainder of your billing cycle.

What payment methods do you accept?

For subscription payments: M-Pesa, Tigo Pesa, Airtel Money, Visa, and Mastercard. For customer payments on your store: All major Tanzanian mobile money providers and international cards. We also support bank transfers for Enterprise customers.

Is there a contract or cancellation fee?

No contracts and no cancellation fees. You can cancel your subscription at any time. If you cancel, you'll continue to have access to your account until the end of your current billing period.

What happens after my free trial ends?

We'll notify you 7 days before your trial ends. You can then choose to continue with the Starter plan, upgrade to Professional, switch to commission-based pricing, or export your data and close your account. No automatic charges will be made without your confirmation.

Do you offer discounts for non-profits or students?

Yes! We offer special pricing for registered non-profit organizations, educational institutions, and student entrepreneurs. Contact our sales team with documentation to learn more about our special programs.
