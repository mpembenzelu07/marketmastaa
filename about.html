Our Journey

From a simple idea to Tanzania's leading digital marketplace

The Problem We Saw

In 2021, we noticed a growing gap in Tanzania's digital economy. Thousands of talented entrepreneurs were creating amazing products but struggled to reach customers beyond their immediate communities.

While social media platforms provided some visibility, they lacked the structure, trust mechanisms, and integrated payment systems needed for serious business growth. Sellers were dealing with payment uncertainties, delivery challenges, and limited market access.

Our Vision

To create a comprehensive digital marketplace that combines global e-commerce best practices with locally-relevant features, making it easy for any Tanzanian business to sell online successfully.

2021

MarketMastaa Founded

Our Mission

To democratize access to digital commerce for every Tanzanian entrepreneur by providing an affordable, secure, and user-friendly platform that enables business growth and economic empowerment.

  • Make digital selling accessible to all
  • Build trust in online transactions
  • Support business growth at every stage

Our Vision

To become East Africa's leading digital marketplace by 2030, connecting millions of buyers and sellers while driving economic growth and digital inclusion across the region.

  • Expand to 5 East African countries
  • Serve 1 million active sellers
  • Process TZS 1 trillion in annual sales
